About MechaFriend
Your Personal Agent

Ever wish you could change people? Add and remove aspects of their personalities? Yeah -- it's not going to work.

However, MechaFriend lets you design your own artificially intelligent agent. It's personality will try to adjust to compatibility with your own. And, you can add personality traits to your liking, including skills that might make your agent better at assisting you.

With MechaFriend, you can literally Make a Friend!
